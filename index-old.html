Building for future generations

The Kyoto blockchain is an ecosystem of green technology solutions, businesses, decentralized applications and communities working towards the common goal of protecting planet earth whilst creating economic opportunities

For individuals

The Kyoto ecosystem can be as passive or inclusive as a user likes. Anybody can passively contribute in the fight against climate change, seek economic opportunity by utilising DeFi services or kickstart an idea into a business with ethical origins.
Join the Kyoto community

For corporations

Corporations can enjoy the same services as individuals with the additional benefit of saving costs when offsetting company commitments or obligations. Kyotoprotocol.io is a full supply chain solution for helping scale the voluntary carbon market.

For climate action

The Kyoto ecosystem implements ReFi into each unique business or dApp helping to fuel climate action. Kyoto tokens can be exchanged directly to offset emissions.
